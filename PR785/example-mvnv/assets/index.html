Multivariate Network Visualization Survey

A companion website for the paper: Evaluating Multivariate Network Visualization Techniques Using a Validated Design and Crowdsourcing Approach

Here you can go through the survey as our crowdsourced participants did, or simply browse through the tasks. Note that survey responses will not be recorded.

You can access the full source code for the study at http://github.com/mvnv-study/survey.


You can find the paper and associated information at https://vdl.sci.utah.edu/publications/2020_chi_mvnv_study/.
