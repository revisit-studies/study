# Introduction

For this study, you will be asked a series of questions about your understanding of health data graphs. These questions will be followed by a demographics section and a section asking about your comfortability with mathematical operations and using graphs to answer questions. This study will take approximately 10-20 minutes. For some questions in this study, you will have to answer on a scale (for example, a scale where 1 = not at all and 6 = very). To answer these questions, click and drag the circle icon either left or right to choose your answer along the scale. When you’re ready to begin, continue on to begin the first section.

For this section, Imagine you recently visited a doctor’s office to discuss medications and had undergone a set of blood tests shortly thereafter. Imagine you are now viewing the results of these tests on an online Electronic Health Records (EHR) portal.
