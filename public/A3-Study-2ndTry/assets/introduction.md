# Introduction

Welcome to our study. This is a replication of [Comparing averages in time series data](https://dl.acm.org/doi/abs/10.1145/2207676.2208556) by Michael Correll, Danielle Albers, Steven Franconeri, and Michael Gleicher.

## Instructions

The following instructions describe the tasks you will see and how to complete them. If you need to refer to these instructions during the experiment, please click on help at the top of the page.

**Task**: In the experiment, you will be shown a series of graphs representing time series data for different months. Each graph will display the average values for each day of the month. Your task is to identify which month has the highest average value based on the graphs provided.
